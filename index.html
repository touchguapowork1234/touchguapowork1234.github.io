Why Tenacity Records?

Artist First Approach

We believe in putting artists at the center of everything we do. Our flexible contracts and fair royalty splits ensure you maintain creative control while receiving the support you deserve.

Global Distribution

Get your music heard worldwide. We distribute to over 150 streaming platforms including Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon Music, and more, reaching millions of potential listeners.

Creative Freedom

Your vision, your sound. We don't force you into a box. Express yourself authentically while we handle the business side, letting you focus purely on your craft.

Marketing Support

From playlist pitching to social media campaigns, our dedicated marketing team works tirelessly to amplify your reach and connect you with your audience.

Professional Production

Access our network of world-class producers, mixing engineers, and mastering studios. We help elevate your sound to industry standards without compromising your artistic vision.

Community & Collaboration

Join a family of like-minded artists. Collaborate, share ideas, and grow together. Our roster is a community that supports and inspires each other.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How do I submit a demo to Tenacity Records?

Click the "Submit Demo" button and fill out our submission form. Include links to your best tracks, social media profiles, and a brief bio about yourself and your music.

What genres does Tenacity Records accept?

We're open to all genres! Whether you make hip-hop, electronic, R&B, pop, rock, or something entirely unique - we want to hear it.

What's the typical contract structure?

We offer flexible, artist-friendly contracts with competitive royalty splits. We believe in transparency and work with each artist to find terms that work for everyone.

Do you provide production services?

Yes! We have a network of talented producers, mixing engineers, and mastering studios. We can help take your music to the next level while maintaining your unique sound.

How long does the demo review process take?

We review all submissions within 3-7 days. If we're interested, we'll reach out to schedule a call and discuss potential next steps.

Where is Tenacity Records based?

We're an independent label with a global mindset. We work with artists from around the world and embrace the digital-first nature of modern music distribution.
